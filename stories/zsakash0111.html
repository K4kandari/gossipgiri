Akash Meets Niharika


Woh academic audit ka din tha jab mai Neharika se pehli baar mila...
Pehli baar main uske pass baithe se daar raha tha( uski bhayanak shakal dekh k ).

Uske kuch din baad hum Room 516 mein mila jaha Rishabh,Kunjika,Reena aur main baith kar baatein kar rahe the.
Tab mujhe pata chala k woh itni bhi bhayanak nai hai.

Kuch din hum logo ki teachers day event k kam se related mulakat hone lagi..

Phir ek din woh humare skit k group mei as a laash(Dead Body) enter hui.
Rehersals ka pehle din toh thik tha. Usko dekh kar laga k ek bahut hi shant aur simple si ladki hai.
Lekin meri yeh galat fehmi Bahut jaldi dur hone wali thi.
Aagle din hum sub rehersal k liye mile, maine aapni zingadi mei pehli baar chudail wali hansi suni uss din.

Woh ek complete alag ladki thi nxt day pagal ki tarah nachna, audi k pardo pe latak k jhulna aur kya kya nahi kiya us ladki ne.

Uss din mujhe realise hua kisi ko bhi first day par judge nai karna chaiye shayad woh insan aapni pagalpanti dikhane k liye warmup ho raha hoo..
